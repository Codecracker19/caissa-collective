About Us

Where every move
is made with intention.

Caissa Collective is a community where chess lovers meet to learn, compete, relax, and enjoy quality coffee in a welcoming environment. We built this space in Kollam because Kerala's chess culture deserved a home — one part club, one part café, entirely built around the game.

Whether you're a first-time player picking up the rules or a rated competitor preparing for your next tournament, Caissa Collective gives you a board, a coach, and a seat at the table. Come for a coffee, stay for a game — or the other way around.
